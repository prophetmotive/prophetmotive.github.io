About NYCETY

NYCety is an application that reads all 311 data submitted for the five boroughs of New York City and visualizes the results on a map of the city. Users allow the app to read their location, and data is overlaid onto a map of the current zip code. The data can be filtered by submission type, date, and issue category, and shows a user what's happening in their area.
